About Procurement Law

Procurement law has become increasingly useful and important in modern day practice in relation to public expenditure. We provide a number of services towards this regard which include advising clients on procurement law, prosecuting and defending clients before the relevant board/ tribunals established under the relevant procurement law. Among the cases successfully litigated in Procurement Law include the award of tender for the construction of Hospitality Buildings at the Kenya School of Monetary Studies by CHINA WU YI LIMITED which involved a sum in excess of Kshs. 5 billion. Award of tender for completion of Nyayo Housing Estate Project, Embakasi in the matter of Tender No.06/2005-2006 National Social Security Fund which involved a sum in excess of Kshs. 600,000,000 to EPCO LIMITED.
